High-Conversion Landing Page: Optimizing Lead Acquisition

"Rediseñé la arquitectura de información para reducir la fricción en el embudo de ventas."

Lead Product Designer
Figma, AI Tools
Conversion & Marketing

Context

The goal was to increase conversions and generate more leads for a high-growth digital service. The existing site struggled to communicate its value proposition clearly, resulting in high bounce rates and low CTA engagement.

The Problem

The previous structure was unclear and lacked a strong, strategic call-to-action. Information layout was cluttered, and the mobile experience was neglected, directly impacting the revenue potential of the campaign.

Visual Insight: Showing the Grid and Typographic Hierarchy. This demonstrates technical design thinking behind the aesthetics.

The Solution

Redesigned the entire layout with a clear hierarchy, simplified sections, and stronger CTA placement. I focused on a "Mobile-First" approach, ensuring the funnel's most critical entry point was perfectly optimized.

  • Strategic UX: Reduced information density by 40%.
  • Conversion Power: Redesigned CTAs using psychology-based visual cues.
  • AI-Driven: Utilized AI workflows for faster asset iteration and high-fidelity mockups.

The Seniority Edge

Accessibility (WCAG)

Contrasts and font sizes are meticulously tested for full accessibility compliance.

Development Ready

Organized handoff files with clear variables and documentation (Dev-Ready).

Micro-interactions

Implemented subtle scroll triggers and button states for a premium, alive feel.

Results

Improved user clarity and significantly increased lead generation. The refined architecture led to a smoother sales funnel and higher stakeholder ROI.
